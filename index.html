Our Heritage

Born from
Passion,
Refined by Time

AURUM was founded with a singular vision: to create jewelry that transcends generations. Each piece is an heirloom waiting to be discovered — handcrafted by master artisans in the tradition of Colombia's legendary goldsmithing heritage.

We source only the finest 18-karat gold and ethically mined gemstones, marrying century-old techniques with contemporary design to create pieces that are as extraordinary as the women who wear them.
